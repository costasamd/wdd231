Our Purpose:

The website will support new players with understanding of the game, announce new tournments and display a library of cards, will also provide a link for the official game's website

Scenarios

What are the rules of the game? is the same rules for all tcg games?

We can assure that some aspects applies to all, like objective: win over your opponent deck, usually by

having a more powerful deck or better strategy. But the answer is no, they have different rules for how you use the cards,

how many cards are allowed in the deck and so on. The best option to learn the tcg you want is to watch tutorials in youtube or refer to the officail manual,

we will link both of them here.

Are you covering all TCG games here?

No, this page is starting with "Yugioh TCG" and we will plan to expand

adding "Pokemon" and "Magic Gathering" as next step, covering all would be virtually impossible,

but we are open for requests, this will take time, but we plan to imcorporate more and more according to demand.
